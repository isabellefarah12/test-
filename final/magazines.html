MAGAZINE

What?

This was a school project in the 1st semester of my freshmen year where we were instructed to create a 16 page interactive magazine on a topic of our choice. As well, we had to generate two commercial adds. We were not required to write the actual content within but just to focus on the layout an aesthetic of the magazine as a whole.

How?

I chose to do my magazine on how to maintain a Healthy Lifestyle. I first chose which articles I wanted to include, then sketched out how each spread would look, and then began creating!
