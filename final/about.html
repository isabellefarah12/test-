Hi! I'm Isabelle

a.k.a IsaVibe — a mixture of Isabelle and the idea of spreading good vibes only! I’m a designer, problem-solver, and creative-thinker. Whether I was coming up with inventions to solve everyday problems as a kid, or making a travel vlog video, I have always had that drive to express myself through visionary ideas. One quote that I feel truly describes me is by Robert Greene: “Creativity is a combination of discipline and childlike spirt.” Having a childlike spirit enables me to look at things with open eyes and feel like anything is possible which combined with my work ethic makes it easy & fun for me to create. Right now, I enjoy social media strategy, designing posters and logo redesign, and coding websites like this one! I was born-and-raised in Fort Lauderdale, Florida and am rising sophomore in the University of Miami’s School of Communications. Other hobbies I have besides being creative, are going on any adventure that fulfills my daredevil side like driving 120mph on a race car speed track at age 16 and edge walking on the 116 story CN Tower, making funny Tik Toks and annoying my furry, adorable puppy Bella!
