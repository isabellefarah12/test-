TRADISYON LAKAY

What?

This was a school project in the 2nd semester of my freshmen year where I was asked to rebrand a non-profit organization that was in need of an aesthetic make over. I was allowed to chose any non-profit organization of my choice as long as it had a problem with their branding and design elements. I had to create a new logo, stationary design, promotional items, and what their social media presence would look like. This was all put into the final product of a brand Guideline book.

How?

To begin this project, I had to conduct research on finding a non-profit that I felt could use a re-design in which I came up with the non-profit Tradisyon Lakay. Then I had brainstorm about elements that represent their brand which I could eventually turn into a new logo. Once I had the logo designed with the its key colors, I was able work on creating all the other design elements that would all communicate the same strong cohesive brand, Traidsyon Lakay.
