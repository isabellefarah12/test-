TOPPEL CAREER CENTER

What?

As the social media intern for the Toppel Career Center at the University of Miami, I not only worked on the social media but also on creating a weekly newsletter. This weekly newsletter, known as the Toppel Insider, would focus on resources, tips, and events that undergraduate students would find useful on their path to becoming career ready.

How?

To put together the Toppel Insider, I would first create a word doc with all the information, then create the canvas to go along with it, and lastly input it into Dreamweaver (coding platform). As well, I would email student nominees along the week to be featured in the Toppel Insider. I always enjoyed crafting the newsletter because it was jamming my creativity and writing into one product!
